## Screenshot Priorities

For ads and store listings, prioritize screenshots that show real merchant value:

1. Dashboard with sales, profit, and low-stock signal.
2. Inventory list with stock levels.
3. Sales/POS entry.
4. Debt tracking.
5. Supplier WhatsApp order.
6. AI Assistant daily actions.
7. Billing and M-Pesa reference flow.

## Usage Notes

- Use Kiswahili-first copy for Tanzanian acquisition.
- Prefer WhatsApp-message campaigns for early paid ads.
- Avoid generic "business software" language; lead with stock, profit, debts, and reordering.
- Keep support and setup promises operationally realistic.
