# WhatsApp Status Launch Pack

Eight vertical 1080x1920 creatives for daily status posts. Upload the PNG or JPG files from `exports/`; keep the SVGs when you need to edit copy or colors.

Suggested order: launch, stock alerts, sales and profit, debts, supplier orders, expenses, AI assistant, then setup CTA. Post one or two per day and end the sequence with the CTA slide.

Keep the caption short: `DukaPilot inakusaidia kuona stock, mauzo, madeni na faida kwa urahisi. Tuma SETUP WhatsApp: +255 743 910 580`.
