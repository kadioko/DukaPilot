## Outreach Workflow

1. Start with High priority only.
2. Confirm the business is active from its website, Instagram, Facebook, or Google listing.
3. Send the personalized WhatsApp message first if WhatsApp/phone is public.
4. Send email where a public business email exists.
5. Record status: contacted, replied, demo booked, registered, activated, paid.
6. Activation target: 10 products, 10 sales, second-day return.

## Personalization Notes

- Supermarkets: lead with stock, sales, and low-stock alerts.
- Hardware: lead with many SKUs, supplier reorders, and fast-moving items.
- Pharmacies: lead with stock visibility and disciplined daily sales records.
- Stationery: lead with high SKU count, school-season demand, and reordering.
- Cosmetics: lead with catalog-like stock, WhatsApp orders, and margin clarity.
- Wholesalers: lead with customer debts, supplier orders, and stock movement.
