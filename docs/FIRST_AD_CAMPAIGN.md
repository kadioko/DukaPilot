# DukaPilot First Ad Campaign

Campaign goal:

> Get WhatsApp conversations with Tanzanian shop owners, not generic website traffic.

## Campaign Setup

Platform:

- Facebook and Instagram.

Objective:

- WhatsApp messages.

Budget:

- TZS 10,000-20,000 per day for 7 days.

Geo:

- Start with Dar es Salaam.
- Expand after response quality is clear.

Audience:

- Small business owners
- Retail
- Wholesale
- M-Pesa
- Supermarket
- Pharmacy
- Cosmetics
- Hardware
- Restaurants / bars

CTA:

- Send WhatsApp message.

Destination:

- `https://wa.me/255743910580?text=Habari%20DukaPilot%2C%20nataka%20setup%20ya%20duka%20langu.%20Aina%20ya%20duka%3A%20`

## Short Video

Length:

- 20-25 seconds.

Format:

- Vertical 9:16.

Recording:

- Phone screen recording plus simple voiceover.

Storyboard:

1. 0-3s: Show notebook or blank paper.
   - Text: `Unajua faida ya leo?`
2. 3-7s: Open DukaPilot dashboard.
   - Text: `Mauzo, stock na faida sehemu moja.`
3. 7-11s: Show sales/POS entry.
   - Text: `Rekodi mauzo kwa sekunde chache.`
4. 11-15s: Show inventory/low stock.
   - Text: `Jua bidhaa zinazoisha.`
5. 15-19s: Show debts or supplier WhatsApp order.
   - Text: `Fuatilia deni na agiza stock.`
6. 19-25s: Show WhatsApp CTA.
   - Text: `Jaribu bure siku 14. Tuma WhatsApp.`

Voiceover:

> Unaendesha duka kwa daftari? DukaPilot hukusaidia kuona stock, mauzo, madeni na faida kwenye simu. Ni Kiswahili, unaanza bure siku 14. Tuma WhatsApp tupange setup ya duka lako.

Caption:

> Mfumo wa duka kwenye simu. Stock, mauzo, madeni na maagizo kwa Kiswahili. Jaribu bure siku 14: WhatsApp +255 743 910 580.

## Test Plan

Run first 7 days with:

- 10 image creatives
- 1 short video
- 5 ad copy variants
- WhatsApp objective only

Track:

- Cost per WhatsApp conversation
- Shop type
- Location
- Setup booked
- Registered
- Activated
- Paid

Kill ads that produce conversations but no setup. Keep ads that produce activated shops.
