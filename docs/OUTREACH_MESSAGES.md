## Sending Rules

- Send only to public business contacts.
- Personalize the shop type and source: "I saw your branch in Masaki" or "I saw your hardware listing."
- Do not send more than 2 follow-ups unless they reply.
- Track every contact in the admin trial notes or a spreadsheet.
- Stop messaging immediately if they ask not to be contacted.
