# Customer Proof Collection

Do not publish a shop name, photo, quote, dashboard image, or business result until the merchant gives clear permission.

## WhatsApp Request

Habari [Jina], asante kwa kutumia DukaPilot. Tunataka kushare uzoefu wa maduka halisi ili kuwasaidia wamiliki wengine kuelewa mfumo. Unaweza kutuma majibu mafupi?

1. Duka lako linauza nini na lipo mji gani?
2. Ni kitu gani DukaPilot imekusaidia kuona au kufanya kwa urahisi?
3. Tunaweza kutumia jina la duka, quote yako, au screenshot iliyofichwa taarifa za wateja kwenye website/WhatsApp/LinkedIn? Jibu: Ndiyo / Hapana.

## Record Before Publishing

- Merchant name and shop name
- Date permission was received
- Approved channels: website, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, ads
- Exact approved quote
- Screenshot approved and customer data redacted
- What result can be claimed, with evidence

## First Three Stories To Collect

1. Grocery or general shop: stock alerts and reordering.
2. Shop selling on credit: debt follow-up and payments.
3. Growing shop: daily sales, expenses, and profit view.

## Public Format

Use this only after approval:

> "[Approved merchant quote]"
>
> [Shop name], [Town] - [shop type]

Keep claims specific and modest. Never turn a merchant's opinion into an unverified performance promise.
